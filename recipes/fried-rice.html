Egg Fried Rice

Description

Alright, so this is gonna be a super simple egg fried rice recipe. This is literally what I cook up real quick for a post workout meal with some chicken breast. Lemme show you how to make it okay?

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup white rice
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • low sodium soy sauce

Steps

  1. Crack your eggs into a bowl and beat em up. Get them nice and mixed.
  2. Heat your pan up to medium heat, add vegetable oil.
  3. Once the pan's nice and hot, add your rice (typically gonna want to use day old rice, but if you only have rice that you just made today it'll do.)
  4. Immediately add your beaten eggs and stir them into the rice, get it nice and mixed together.
  5. Add soy sauce to rice, just eyeball it. You want your rice to look brown-ish.
  6. Lastly, drizzle your little bit of sesame oil over the rice and stir it in. Sesame oil rules, just don't use too much.
  7. And you're good to go! Plate your rice up and enjoy. :)
